Life Cycle

Giraffes begen their lives as eggs, it is currently unknown where exactly these eggs come from, but it has been theorized that round stones, spontaneously transmogrify into them during harvest moons. After roughly 14 monthes the eggs hatch, revealing the furtive pupa. upon hatching these pupae emediatly bury themselves in the surrounding soil, and feed off the minerals within for another 7 months. At this point giraffes emerge and shed there skin, finally becoming the lovable goliaths we all know.
