Rituals

Giraffes have a number of odd rituals and celebrations, but many share common themes of great remorse and autocannibalism. In many cases these rituals continue until at least one participant has died, or collaped in grief. Not participating is seen as a social fopah worthy of death. However, executions too follow these rules. This has lead to several notable examples in which executions have chained together for a number of months
