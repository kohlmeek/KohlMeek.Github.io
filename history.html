History

Giraffes first entered the public eye in 1964, when a giraffe entrepeuner struck oil in his central Tenesse back yard. From there he built a large fortune and helped support giraffe culture and historical studies with several large grants and donations to museums across the world. His work is what allows us to know as much as we do.
