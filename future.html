Future

In recent years giraffes have died in great numbers due to a mix of penstilence, poching, deforistation, and use of new insect repellents like agent orange. In light of this it is somewhat unclear what the future may hold for giraffes. Preservation efforts are being made around the globe, but these attempts are uniformly met with by hostility in giraffe communities.
